My Education Background

Primary School

I attended primary school at Sekolah Kebangsaan Pedah which is located in Jerantut district which is one of the districts in Pahang. I went to school there for 6 years from 2007 to 2012.

High School

After graduating from primary school. I continued my studies in high school. I went to a girls' boarding school in Bandar Kota Bharu, Kelantan. The name of the school is Maahad Muhammadi Perempuan. I went to school there for 5 years, from 2013 to 2017.

Diploma in Information Management

After finishing school in 2017, I was offered to further my studies at Universiti Teknologi Mara in the field of information management. Therefore, I continued my studies at the Diploma in Information Management at Uitm Segamat.

Bachelor of Information Management (Hons.) Record Management

After completing the diploma program at the end of 2020, I continued my studies to the Degree in the same field which is Bachelor of Information Management (Hons.) Record Management. Currently, I am in semester 4 and am expected to graduate in 2022.
